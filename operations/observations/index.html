EXPLICIT NON-CLAIMS

An observation is not authorization

A signed observation does not prove contract safety, bytecode identity, reserve state, finality, deployment correctness, chain availability, or production approval.

It does not accept transactions, forward public RPC, expose private mesh information, or replace a local policy decision.
